Artificial intelligence (AI) has transformed the way information is created and shared, but it has also intensified the spread of mis- and disinformation. As Cambodia prepares its Draft National AI Strategy (2025-2030), information integrity is an element that cannot be neglected. It is against this backdrop that the panel discussion on “AI and Disinformation on digital platforms” took place.
